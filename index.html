How to add a new roadmap

Four steps to extend this collection

1

Create data/your-slug-data.js — define TOPICS, PHASE_LABELS, and optionally PHASE_ORDER.

2

Add a card in index.html linking to details.html?data=your-slug. No other HTML changes needed.

3

script.js auto-detects the slug from the URL, loads data/your-slug-data.js, and renders everything dynamically.

4

Pick an accent color and badge styles for your card. Progress is saved per-roadmap in localStorage.
